Chicken Spaghetti

Description

Savory chicken spaghetti casserole made with cheddar cheese, cream of mushroom soup, and topped with bread crumbs

Ingredients

  • 1 whole raw or rotisserie chicken, cut into small pieces
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 1/2 cups shredded sharp cheddar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 cans cream of mushroom soup
  • Bread crumbs as needed

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 F
  2. If the chicken is raw, you will need to boil, cook, and cut it.
  3. Cook spaghetti
  4. Once spaghetti is cooked, combine with the chicken, cream of mushroom soup, cheese, and rest of ingredients.
  5. Make sure the casserole is covered in cheese, and top with bread crumbs, then bake in the oven for around 45 minutes.
  6. Now enjoy!
