Green Spaghetti

Description

A mexican style spaghetti made with roasted poblano peppers, garlic, cilantro, onions, and more. This dish can be made spicy by adding serrano peppers or left at a mild level with just the poblano peppers.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 4 poblano peppers
  • 1 cup cilantro
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 box cream cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 3 serrano peppers (optional)
  • Cotija cheese

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the spaghetti while completing the rest of the steps.
  2. Roast the poblano peppers (in an oven, on the stove, in an air fryer).
  3. Place the roasted peppers to steam for 5-10 minutes in a zip-loc or grocery bag sealed well.
  4. After steaming the peppers, you now must peel the skin, and de-vein them and cut into strips.
  5. If you choose to use serrano peppers you should roast and steam those as well, but de-veining is not necessary.
  6. Place the peppers, cilantro, onion, cheeses, garlic, and water in a blender until creamy and smooth.
  7. Pour sauce into a large pot and simmer on a low temp adding salt and pepper as needed. Once the sauce is at a simmer you can add the spaghetti and stir.
  8. All done. Enjoy!
